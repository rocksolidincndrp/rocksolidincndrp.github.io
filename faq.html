What do you do?

Rock Solid is a Web Development and Graphic Design business, aimed at providing customers custom graphics and websites for personal or business use.

Do you do personal work?

Yes, we can create work for personal and indivudal use.

How are the websites made?

Our websites are build by hand, from the ground up using HTML, CSS and JavaScript. None of the work you see being published by Rock Solid is created using website editors like Wix or WordPress.

How are the websites hosted?

Rock Solid does NOT provide webhosting or domains for you to use. While we have free solutions, such as GitHub Pages used to host most of our websites so far, we do not provide hosting solutions. If you so decide, at project complition you will recieve your website files and are able to host the website however you may please.

How does maintenance/retainer agreement work?

Our maintance contract is payed on a monthly basis and offers full support for website maintance, it does not include full reworks. Examples can be: adding new items you sell, small updates for seasonal themes, updated advertisments or banners on your website. Esentially, we offer to keep your website up to date, within reason.

What services do you offer for graphic design?

Our graphic design services include but are not limited to: Business Cards, Adverts, Posters, Album Covers, Logos and Thumbnails.
