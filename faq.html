How are the websites made?

Our websites are built by hand, from the ground up, using HTML, CSS and JavaScript. None of the work you see being published by Rock Solid was created using website editors like Wix or WordPress.

How are the websites hosted?

Rock Solid does NOT provide web hosting or domains for you to use. While we have free solutions, such as GitHub Pages used to host most of our websites so far, we do not provide hosting solutions. If you so decide, at project completion you will receive your website files and are able to host the website however you may please.

How does maintenance/retainer agreement work?

Our maintenance contract is paid on a monthly basis and offers full support for website maintenance; it does not include full reworks. Examples can be: adding new items you sell, small updates for seasonal themes; updated advertisements or banners on your website. Essentially, we offer to keep your website up to date, within reason.

What services do you offer for graphic design?

Our graphic design services include, but are not limited to: Business Cards, Advertisements, Posters, Album Covers, Logos and Thumbnails.
